I'm Mansoor Ahmed

I am passionate about data analytics, machine learning, and bioinformatics with a focus on neuroimaging. I currently work as a Research Assistant jointly at the Data Analytics lab (affilited with NCBC) and Smart Data Systems and Applications (SDSA) lab at LUMS. My broad research areas are the identification of biomarkers for neurological disorders and the computational design of antibodies using sequences. Specifically, my current research projects are related to the estimation of brain age using machine learning and deep learning techniques on a large collection of brain MRIs. I develop workflows for processing MRIs by applying image processing techniques using Neuroimaging tools such as FSL, FreeSurfer, and CAT12 and prepare the tabular data for subsequent analysis. I am ambitious to further advance my research capabilites in AI for medical-related application, specifically in medical imaging.
