Research Projects and Publications

Exploring the Impact of Embedding Methods on Graph-based Antibody-aware Epitope Prediction (Presented at ICCABS-2025) - [BioRxiv, Slides, GitHub]

  • Designed and implemented a graph neural network (GCN) framework to predict antibody-antigen interactions using 3D structural data, leveraging protein language models (PLMs) like AntiBERTy, ESM2, and ProtBERT for residue embeddings. This approach significantly improved epitope prediction accuracy over traditional sequence-based methods.
  • Contribution: Achieved state-of-the-art performance on the AsEP benchmark dataset, with a 0.263 MCC and 0.348 F1 score, outperforming existing methods like GraphBepi and EpiGraph.

Robust Brain Age Estimation via Regression Models and MRI-derived Features (ICCCI-2023) - [ Paper, Slides, GitHub]

  • Integrated the region-wise volumetric measurements of 3965 healthy individuals derived from T1-weighted MRIs and trained regression models to estimate brain age.
  • Result: Achieved an MAE of 3.25 years.

Mitigating Covariance in MRI Site-effects in a Federated Learning Framework (ongoing) - [ Slides, GitHub]

  • Developing a decentralized algorithm for harmonizing multi-site MRI data using region-based brain features of 4k healthy controls, collected across 64 sites.
  • Contribution: The first MRI harmonizing approach in a federated setting that removes bias in covariance between features, achieving accuracy equivalent to centralized approaches.

A Data-driven Pipeline for Designing Antibodies (ongoing) - [ Slides]

  • Developing the first-of-its-kind end-to-end computational pipeline for designing antibodies for a given antigen sequence using advanced bioinformatics, machine learning, and NLP methods.

Deep Feature Ensemble-based Brain Age Estimation - [Github]

  • Developed a brain age estimation (BAE) framework by ensembling 3D CNN, ResNet, Vision Transformer, and AlexNet using the T1-weighted 3D MRI volumes of 3966 healthy controls.
  • Result: Achieved state-of-the-art with an MAE of 2.05 years.

Liquidity is all you need: A decentralized approach to generative agentic AI systems with liquidity - [Github]

  • Designed and implemented a simulation modeling the behavior of diverse platform participants (creators, buyers, investors, speculators) in a multi-token economy, incorporating heterogeneity, bounded rationality, learning, and network effects.
  • Conducted in-depth analysis of platform properties such as price discovery, supply-demand trends, volatility, and arbitrage opportunities, while investigating the impact of bonding curve shapes and agent behaviors on system dynamics.
  • Generated simulation outputs to support explanatory, exploratory, and predictive tasks, enabling the forecasting of platform behavior under varying parameters and agent strategies.

Quantifying the Impact of Brain Tumor on the Brain Age

  • Developed an improved brain age estimation (BAE) model using the region-wise, surface-wise, and voxel-wise features extracted from 757 healthy MRIs with an MAE of 3.1 years.
  • Result: Predicted the brain age of brain tumor patients using our BAE model and observed an increased brain age gap of 10.3 years in tumor patients compared to healthy controls.

A Deep Learning-based Interpretable Framework for Early-stage Alzheimer's Disease Detection

  • Developed a CNN-based model for detecting early-stage Alzheimer's Disease (AD) and interpreted the aging-related phenomenon using multimodal input data of 457 samples from the ADNI dataset.
  • Contribution: The novel approach combines data from multiple modalities and provides explainability of the early-stage changes in the brain of AD patients.
